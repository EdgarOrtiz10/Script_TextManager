STEP FAMILY
In actuality, the society has had changes and challenges what this gets new family types, one of this type is the step family....
The positive things of the step family are:
•	Two sources of income can improve financial situation of couple (Dos fuentes de ingresos pueden mejorar la situación financiera de la pareja)
•	This can improve the quality of life of people (Esto puede mejorar la calidad de vida de las personas)
•	When the parents are happy, the children will happy (Cuando los padres están felices, los niños estarán felices)
•	With a new lifestyle, there will be new attitudes (Con un nuevo estilo de vida, habrá nuevas actitudes.)
•	Their partners may solve problems for children or siblings (Sus parejas pueden resolver problemas para niños o hermanos)
•	The children will not feel alone (Los niños no se sentiran solos)
•	The children may get new siblings to play with

But this type family also they have big negative impact, for example:
•	the children may not accept the new partner (los niños pueden no aceptar a la nueva pareja)
•	There may be conflict between children and partner
•	Emotions problems for children with new partners
•	The partners have different parenting styles may lead to problems (Los socios tienen diferentes estilos de crianza que pueden generar problemas.)
•	The children there may be problems in the school or university.
•	The children may not listen to their new partners.
